About Suraj Gattani

I'm a Senior ML Scientist at Schrodinger with a passion for applying cutting-edge machine learning techniques to solve complex problems in molecular discovery and drug development. My journey from Electronics Engineering to the forefront of AI and bioinformatics has been driven by a constant curiosity about how technology can accelerate scientific breakthroughs.

My Mission

I believe in the transformative power of artificial intelligence to revolutionize how we understand and manipulate molecular structures. My work focuses on developing state-of-the-art machine learning models that can predict molecular properties with unprecedented accuracy, ultimately accelerating the discovery of new drugs and materials.

Technical Philosophy

I approach every problem with a combination of deep technical expertise and practical business acumen. Whether it's architecting scalable ML pipelines that generate significant revenue or developing novel graph neural networks that push the boundaries of molecular property prediction, I focus on creating solutions that are both scientifically rigorous and commercially viable.

Beyond Work

When I'm not developing ML models, you can find me exploring the latest advances in generative AI, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring the next generation of data scientists. I'm also passionate about making AI more accessible and understandable through technical writing and speaking engagements.
